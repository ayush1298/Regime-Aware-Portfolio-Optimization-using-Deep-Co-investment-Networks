Three-Stage Pipeline

Co-investment Networks

An LSTM-based model learns shared latent weight structures across S&P 500 stocks. These hidden connections form a yearly co-investment graph that reveals how institutional portfolios move together — far richer than simple price correlations.

Explore Networks

Regime Detection

Graph-theoretic features (density, clustering, path length, assortativity, entropy) are extracted each year and fed to a Hidden Markov Model to classify the market into Bull, Crisis, or Recovery regimes — matched {{ kpis.regime_matches }}/{{ kpis.regime_total }} known events.

View Regimes

Adaptive Portfolio

The regime-aware strategy dynamically switches between hub-follow (growth) and hub-avoid (defensive) centrality-based portfolios depending on the detected regime. Backtested against S&P 500 equal-weight and PCC baselines from 2011 to 2016.
